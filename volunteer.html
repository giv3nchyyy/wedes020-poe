Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need gardening experience?

No! We welcome volunteers of all skill levels. We provide training and guidance for all our garden projects.

What's the time commitment?

We have flexible opportunities ranging from 2 hours per week to monthly weekend projects.

Are there age restrictions?

Volunteers must be 16+. Those under 18 need parental consent. School projects are great for student volunteers!

Do you accept corporate groups?

Yes! We love corporate volunteer days. Contact us to arrange a team-building garden project.
