About Green Futures Initiative

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team driving change

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate environmental science students from the University of Cape Town, Green Futures Initiative began as a response to the growing disconnect between urban youth and their natural environment.

What started as a weekend volunteer project at three local schools has blossomed into a movement spanning 50+ schools across the Western Cape. Our founders recognized that many children in under-resourced communities had limited access to practical environmental education and fresh, nutritious food.

Today, we continue to grow, driven by our belief that every child deserves the opportunity to connect with nature and learn skills that will sustain them for life.

Our Mission

To empower communities through hands-on environmental education and the creation of sustainable food gardens in schools, fostering a generation of environmentally conscious leaders.

Our Vision

A future where every young South African has the knowledge, skills, and opportunity to be an active guardian of their environment and contribute to local food security.
